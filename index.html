About

I'm a Senior Platform Engineer based in Florida, working fully remote, specializing in internal developer platforms, cloud-native infrastructure, and GitOps automation. My work sits at the intersection of what developers need to ship confidently and what operations teams need to sleep at night.

Before moving into DevOps, I spent 8 years in application support and operations — which means I understand production systems from the inside out. I don't just build pipelines; I build systems that are operable, documented, and designed to be handed off cleanly.

Currently at Trimble, where I own the full platform stack: Kubernetes (EKS), ArgoCD, Concourse CI, and Confluent. I'm actively deepening my work in AI-augmented platform tooling — building smarter internal workflows with the latest generation of LLMs and automation tools.

I hold a BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Miami and am currently pursuing AWS Solutions Architect and CKA certifications.
