About

I'm a Senior DevOps Engineer based in Florida, working fully remote, specializing in platform engineering, cloud-native infrastructure, and GitOps automation. My work sits at the intersection of what developers need to ship confidently and what operations teams need to sleep at night.

Before moving into DevOps, I spent 8 years in application support and operations — which means I understand production systems from the inside out. I don't just build pipelines; I build systems that are operable, documented, and designed to be handed off cleanly.

Currently I own the full platform stack: Kubernetes (EKS), ArgoCD, Concourse CI, Kong Gateway, Kafka (migrating from Confluent to AWS MSK), and Terraform-based infrastructure provisioning across multiple AWS accounts and environments. I'm also actively integrating AI-augmented tooling into platform workflows — using AI-assisted IaC development, agentic code review, and MCP integrations to accelerate infrastructure delivery.

I hold a BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Miami and am pursuing AWS Solutions Architect and CKA certifications.
